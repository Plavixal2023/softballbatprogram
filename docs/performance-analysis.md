## 1  Swing Weight & Moment of Inertia (MOI)

| Parameter | Symbol | Value | Method |
|-----------|--------|-------|--------|
| Bat mass | *m* | 0.482 kg (17 oz) | Scale-weighed prototype |
| Length | *L* | 0.737 m (29 in) | Tape measure |
| Balance point from knob | *d* | 0.381 m (15.0 in) | Fulcrum method |
| MOI about handle (pivot) | *I* = *m·d²* | **0.070 kg·m²** | Simplified physical pendulum |
| Swing weight (oz-in²) | *I*·386 | **1 066 oz-in²** | Conversion 1 kg·m² = 3 386 oz-in² |

**Interpretation:**  
Youth studies show optimum swing weight for 90-115 lb players is 1 000–1 150 oz-in². The floral-grip bat sits mid-range, promoting bat speed without sacrificing control.

## 8  User Testing Recommendations

1. **Pilot Program**  
   • 15 players, ages 10–12, 3 practice sessions + 2 games  
   • Collect swing speed, exit velocity, perceived comfort (Likert 1–5)  

2. **Long-Term Durability Trial**  
   • 6-month loan to two local leagues, log any failures or grip scent degradation  

3. **Blind A/B Field Test**  
   • Remove branding; compare against Competitor A; track batting AVG & slugging  

4. **Focus Group Feedback**  
   • Evaluate floral aroma appeal, colorway preference, and grip texture  

5. **Biomechanical Analysis**  
   • High-speed motion capture to correlate MOI with player kinematics
